# Advanced Geophysical Methods in Natural Resource Investigation (ANRI)

**Module:** 14506
**Programme:** Master Environmental and Resource Management (PO 2021)
**Department:** Faculty 2 — Environment and Natural Sciences, BTU Cottbus-Senftenberg
**Responsible:** Prof. Dr. rer. nat. Rainer Herd
**Language:** English
**Credits:** 6 ECTS | **Duration:** 1 semester | **Max participants:** 15

---

## About This Course

This course provides advanced training in applied geophysical methods for the exploration and investigation of natural resources. Students acquire theoretical foundations and practical skills in a range of near-surface and airborne geophysical techniques, with emphasis on data acquisition, processing, interpretation, and integration within environmental and resource management contexts.
