## Exam Modalities

### Written Exam
- Duration: 90 minutes
- Closed book; one hand-written formula sheet (A4, both sides) permitted
- Covers all course units
- Mix of short-answer, calculation, and interpretation questions

### Field/Project Report
- Produced in groups of 2–3 students
- Must include: introduction, methods, data processing, results, interpretation, and conclusions
- Submission in PDF format via the course platform
- Plagiarism policy: BTU academic integrity rules apply

### Oral Presentation
- Individual presentation of a topic assigned during the semester
- 15 minutes presentation + 5 minutes Q&A
- Evaluated on: technical accuracy, clarity, and ability to respond to questions
