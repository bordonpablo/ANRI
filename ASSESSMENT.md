# Assessment — Advanced Geophysical Methods in Natural Resource Investigation (ANRI)

**Module:** 14506 | **Credits:** 6 ECTS
**Responsible:** Dr. Pablo Bordón

---

## Assessment Components

| Component | Weight | Format |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| Written Exam | 60% | Closed-book, 90 minutes |
| Field/Project Report | 30% | Group report, written |
| Oral Presentation | 10% | Individual, 15 minutes |

> Specific deadlines and submission details are published each semester under `Semesters/<semester>/Assignments/`.

---

## Exam Modalities

### Written Exam
- Duration: 90 minutes
- Closed book; one hand-written formula sheet (A4, both sides) permitted
- Covers all course units
- Mix of short-answer, calculation, and interpretation questions

### Field/Project Report
- Produced in groups of 2–3 students
- Must include: introduction, methods, data processing, results, interpretation, and conclusions

### Oral Presentation
- Individual presentation of a topic assigned during the semester
- 15 minutes presentation + 5 minutes Q&A

---

## Attendance Policy

Regular attendance is expected. Students missing more than 20% of sessions without justified reason may be excluded from the final exam. Field excursions are mandatory.
